Pink Sauce Pasta

Description

The greatest pasta recipe known to mankind! This would even make top chefs jealous!

Ingredients

  • Pasta (Preferrably Penne or Spaghetti , but any type would do)
  • Olive Oil
  • Chicken Breast (how much ever you need)
  • 1 - 2 cans of tomato sauce
  • 1 - 2 packets of whole cooking cream
  • Cajun Spice Powder
  • Tomatoes
  • Red and Green Peppers
  • Enough water to fill a pot
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Fill a pot with water and bring it to a boil
  2. Whist the water boils, cut up the chicken into small pieces and spread the cajun mixture over it
  3. Next, cut up the tomatoes and peppers into small chunks
  4. Then, heat up a pan to high heat and pour oil in it
  5. By this time, the water will be boiling. Add the pasta into the boiling water and set a timer for 8 minutes (or till the pasta becomes aldente)
  6. Sprinkle a lot of salt onto the boiling water
  7. Whilst the pasta gets cooked, add the chicken into the pan and stir till cooked
  8. Once the chicken is done, add the veggies and stir for about a minute
  9. Then add the sauces (tomato and cream) into the pan and mix in low heat
  10. By this time, the pasta must be ready. Before straining the pasta, add some pasta water to the sauce so that it doesn't get too thick
  11. Strain the pasta and add it to the sauce
  12. Mix for a few minutes before it is ready to serve

Now you are ready to feast upon this creamy heaven!

Make sure that the pasta is properly salted and cooked before eating!
