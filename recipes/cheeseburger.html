Cheeseburger

Description

There can be no burger recipe better than this right here! Don't make the mistake of missing out on this marvel!

Ingredients

  • 2 brioche buns (any bun would do)
  • 2 beef patties (don't know the proportions; just needs to be fresh ground beef)
  • Extra Virgin Olive Oil (any type of oil would do)
  • Salt and Pepper
  • Butter
  • A packet of American/Cheddar cheese
  • Ketchup
  • Mayo
  • Hot sauce
  • Relish
  • 1 Onion

Steps

  1. Heat up a pan to high heat
  2. While the pan is being heated, take your beef patties and season them generously with salt and pepper on both sides
  3. Pour the oil onto the pan and place both patties on top of the pan on one side (sepearate obviously)
  4. Wait for about a minute till the bottom gets cooked
  5. While waiting, cut up a few thin slices of onion and cook them in a seperate hot pan with a few slices of butter till caramelized.
  6. Once the patties are done cooking on one side, flip them and place two slices of cheese on top of each patty and wait till they're melted
  7. While the patties cook, take the onions out and toast the insides of each bun slice with the remaining butter.
  8. In the meantime, take 2 tbsp of ketchup and mayo, a teaspoon of hot sauce and 1/2 tbsp of relish to make the special sauce
  9. Once everything is done cooking, assemble the burger in the following manner:
    1. Spread a touch of the special sauce on the base of each bun slice
    2. Add the patty on top of the base bun
    3. Top the patty with the onion
    4. Complete the burger with the bun top on top

And that's it you're done!

Enjoy this delicious burger after a hard day of work!
